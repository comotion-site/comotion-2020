ABOUT

CoMotion is a student-lead motion graphics conference that brings together top industry professionals with Motion Media Design students at the Savannah College of Art and Design.

The event includes a keynote speech, panel discussions, the annual curated and juried Student Showcase, portfolio reviews, and an awards ceremony.
